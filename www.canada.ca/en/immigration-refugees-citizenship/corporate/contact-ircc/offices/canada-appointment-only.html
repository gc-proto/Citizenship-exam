IRCC offices in Canada – By appointment only

Depending on your application, we may contact you to schedule an appointment at one of the offices listed on this page.

To make a refugee claim, bring your completed application to one of these offices. Not all of them accept refugee claims and some only accept them during specific hours. Check the list before you pick an office to bring your application to.

We also have temporary offices in different regions across Canada. These services include:

  • citizenship tests,
  • citizenship interviews (hearings with a citizenship judge or interviews with a citizenship officer),
  • citizenship ceremonies, and
  • immigration interviews (including Permanent Resident (PR) Card distribution).

Visit our help centre for quick answers to common questions.

If you need to contact us about your application, use this Web form.
