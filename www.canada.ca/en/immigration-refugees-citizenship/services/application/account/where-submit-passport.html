Where to submit your passport for your online application

Below are instructions intended only for applicants who have submitted their application online via their account and who have received an email requesting that they submit their passport to continue the processing of their application.

Do not send your passport or any document unless we have contacted you, as this could delay the processing of your application.

Determine where to send your passport

To determine where to send your passport, you will need to refer to the answers you provided to questions 7 and 9 on your application form with regards to your country of residence and your country of application. 

If you do not have a copy of your completed and validated application form, it can be accessed through your account.

To access your application in your account
  1. Log into your account.
  2. On the welcome page, under “what would you like to do today”, navigate to the “View my submitted applications” section.
  3. Click on “Check status and messages”.
  4. On the “Application Details” page, click on “view submitted application”.
  5. Your application will open in a new window.

In your application form, if you have answered:

  1. Yes to question 9, use the country/territory you provided to question 7
  2. No to question 9, use the country/territory you provided to question 9

Click on the name of the appropriate country/territory in the alphabetical list below.

Note that the instructions for the submission of your passport may be different than the instructions on visa office websites. Instructions on visa office websites are meant for applicants not using e-Apps. Follow the instructions provided on this page when submitting/sending your passport to avoid delays.

When you submit your passport, it is important to include the letter we have sent to your account.

Submitting your passport at a drop box or in person

Some visa offices offer drop box or in person collection services to applicants. Note that if you choose to use a drop box or in person service, same-day return of passport may not always be possible.

Visa office security

Please note that there are security protocols particular to each visa office. Generally, luggage, such as backpacks, suitcases or packages larger than a standard handbag, are not allowed into our offices (Embassy, Consulate or High-Commission), and applicants are discouraged from bringing mobile phones or other electronic devices.  More precise instructions on these protocols can be found on each visa office websites.
