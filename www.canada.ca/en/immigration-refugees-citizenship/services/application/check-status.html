Check your application status

You may be able to check the status of your immigration or citizenship application online. It depends on what you applied for.

Need Help?

If you can’t check your status online, you can use processing times as a guide to find out how long it might take to process your application.

Answer the questions below to find out which one applies to you.

What did you apply for?

  • Immigration
  • Family sponsorship
  • Citizenship
  • Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)
  • Visitor visa
  • Work permit
  • Study permit
  • Permanent resident card (PR Card)
  • Refugees
  • Verification of status (VOS) or request to amend

Immigration

This includes applications for:

  • federal skilled workers
  • federal skilled trades
  • provincial nominees
  • caregivers
  • Canadian Experience Class
  • Quebec-selected skilled workers
  • temporary resident permits
  • protected persons
  • humanitarian and compassionate cases
  • Quebec investors
  • Quebec entrepreneurs
  • self-employed persons
  • Start-up visas
  • Immigrant Investor Venture Capital Pilot Program

Did you apply to Canada’s former Immigrant Investor or Entrepreneur Programs?

Your application may have been cancelled. See how you may be affected and how you can get your fees refunded.

Online applications

If you applied online, sign in to your account to check your application status. Click “check status and messages” under “View my submitted applications or profiles.”

Sign in to your account

Paper applications

There are two ways to check your application status online. You can:

  • create an online account and link your paper application to it. You’ll get:
    • detailed application status in real-time and
    • email alerts and messages online about your application instead of by mail, or
  • use the online tool, which is updated daily.

Not all people who applied on paper can check their status through an online account. See which applications you can link to your online account.

Processing times

If you can't check your application status online, you can check processing times to see how long it takes to process your type of application.

Check processing times

Find out more about what happens after you submit your application.

  • orphans
  • other family classes

Paper applications

There are two ways to check your application status online.

If you are the person being sponsored, you can:

  • create an online account and link your paper application to it. You’ll get:
    • detailed application status in real-time and
    • email alerts and messages online about your application instead of by mail, or
  • use the online tool, which is updated daily.

Both you and the person sponsoring you can check the status of your application by using our online tool.

Processing times

If you cannot use the check application status tool, you can check processing times to see how long it takes to process your type of application.

Check processing times

Find out more about what happens after you submit your application.
